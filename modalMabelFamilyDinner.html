2014 Mabel Family Dinner

Part of the national Family Dinner Project, a group of school teachers and administrators from Mabel Elementary school organized and hosted a community family dinner (hopefully the first of many to come). On November 14, 2014, Mabel Elementary School hosted their fist community family dinner. Green Street Catering served 110 people with a large buffet. Participants came together to enjoy community fellowship and were encouraged to have regular family meals together at home. For more information, please visit School Counselor Newsletter for more information.
