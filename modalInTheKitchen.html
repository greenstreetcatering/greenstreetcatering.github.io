Weekly community meals

Every Thursday, Green Street Catering offers a free community meal to anyone that it can benefit. Our team of volunteers prepares and delivers an average of 400 meals each week. Since October 2010, we have served and delivered over 70,000 meals.

Green Street works hard throughout the week to gather fresh foods from generous businesses/organizations. These fresh fruits, vegetables, breads and even desserts are organized and prepared into a well-balanced and nutritious meal every Thursday. Some individuals pick up meals for themselves. Many meals are delivered to homes by partnering churches, small groups and even individuals who also receive meals for themselves and their own families.

People come from all different backgrounds to serve the needs of the community. Likewise, people come from all different backgrounds seeking a hot and healthy meal. Green Street Catering is a place for people helping people.
