Services

Food, Wellness and Community

Food

Air, water, shelter, clothing and food – five basic essentials needed by every human for survival and elements that we hope are fully available to every member of our community. There are several organizations that try to help meet the needs of any person lacking in one of those fundamental elements needed to sustain us. Green Street Catering, a local, not for profit, food service organization, was born out of the desire that no one in our community lack that fifth essential – food.

Green Street offers a delicious, hot, home-cooked meal on Thursday evenings, to any and all that would like one, regardless of means or circumstance. In addition, Green Street works alongside other local church groups and organization to enhance their own service programs, and handling the burden of food preparation, allowing the group to focus on meeting the other physical, emotional and spiritual needs of those they are intent on serving.

Wellness

Green Street, in an effort to broaden our reach and impact, is embarking on an ambitious new program entitled NEED. NEED stands for Nutrition, Education, Eating and Diet

Green Street’s NEED program will provide food baskets, free weekly community meals and valuable nutritional education to Watauga County. The program will also include seminars on a wide variety of topics ranging from shopping and cooking strategies to how to feed your growing family.

Our goal is to provide valuable insight into ways that participants can maintain a healthy balanced diet along without loosing a balanced budget.

NEED will also provide free doctor’s consultations, offering participants an opportunity to have their blood pressure and other vital indicators checked and be able to ask questions and receive advice from qualified local healthcare professionals without the normal costs associated with such visits. Our volunteer health and wellness professionals include physicians, dentists, nurses, physical therapists, trainers and others. In addition to consultations, NEED will also offer some basic dental education and care.

Need will work to empower more residents, through our awareness and education programs to eat, shop and cook in a healthier way for their families. NEED is putting great importance on building relationships with other social services in our community to strengthen our support systems in our county.

If you are interested in helping with the NEED program, please contact Executive Director, Chastity Lesesne at 919.360.4326 or email us at greenstreetcatering@gmail.com.

We have opportunities to serve in a wide variety of manners, from offering financial support to cooking and delivering meals, to fostering relationships with those that we serve.
